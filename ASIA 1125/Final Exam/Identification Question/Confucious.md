* 551 - 479 BCE
* Born in Qufu
* Known as Kongzi, Master Kong during his lifetime 
* Was born to a man who had the rank of knight (man of service) - the lowest rank in nobility (served as an aid to major aristocratic family)
* Mother was a concubine and he grew with her family (he was three when his father died)
* He held low ranking positions managing accounts and watching over livestock
