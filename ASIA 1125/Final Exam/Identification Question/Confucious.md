Confucius was a philosopher who lived in the state of Lu during the time of Spring and Autumn period of 551 - 479 BCE.  During that time Confucious was also known as Kongzi or Master Kong, he promoted "The Way  (known as Dao)", which was a extension of the means of emphasizing principles like personal, justice, and sincerity. At that time Confucius started his career as a tutor for young men who were interested in learning the art of governance, at the same time he also wanted to pursue as a advisor to a ruler.
