* 551 - 479 BCE
* Born in Qufu
* Known as Kongzi, Master Kong during his lifetime 
* Was born to a man who had the rank of knight (man of service) - the lowest rank in nobility (served as an aid to major aristocratic family)
* Mother was a concubine and he grew with her family (he was three when his father died)
* He held low ranking positions managing accounts and watching over livestock

Confucius was a philosopher who lived in the state of Lu during the time of Spring and Autumn period of 551 - 479 BCE.  During that time Confucius was also known as Kongzi or Master Kong, he promoted "The Way  (known as Dao)", which was a extension of the means of emphasizing principles like personal, justice, and sincerity. He modified the concept of Li ritual from a set of ceremonies to an abstract quality that could be applied to all social interaction and in turn was able to teach self cultivation and were able to embody ren.  Confucius started his career as a tutor for young men who were interested in learning the art of governance, at the same time he also wanted to pursue as a advisor to a ruler. In the early periods, his early students had faced restraint during the Qin dynasty but later on it during the Han dynasty it gained a lot of historical significance when they endorsed Confucianism as the national philosophy under Emperor Wu in 140 -87 BCE.
