Confucian Recruitment/Education:
    - Wu named five scholars as **Erudite Scholars** specializing in Confucian classics, a step toward forming the Confucian canon.
    - Named 50 students to study with the scholars and created an **imperial academy**.
    - Established **schools in each locality**; students could join local government, attend the imperial academy, or be appointed to the central bureaucracy.
    - The Han dynasty continued to recruit officials by **recommendation**, requiring them to take **examinations** upon arrival in the capital for appropriate placement.
